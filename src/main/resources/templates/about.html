About Us

What we do

We are a card shop that sells high quality trading cards from games such as Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and Pokemon. We also sell other table-top gaming products such as board games, along with card gaming peripherals such as card sleeves, deck boxes, and playmats.

We also run many events including card gaming tournaments such as Friday Night Magic, and tournaments for other trading card games. We also run events for other table-top games such as Dungeons and Dragons.
